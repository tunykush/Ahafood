Premium Package Insurance Policy

AhaFood.AI is committed to comprehensive support and protecting the interests of restaurants using the Premium package when revenue significantly declines. This transparent and fair policy helps you grow your business sustainably.

1. Eligibility

Available for restaurants using the Premium package with at least 4 months of continuous activity on the AhaFood.AI platform.

2. Insurance Period

  • 6-month Premium package: 1 month insurance coverage
  • 12-month Premium package: 3 months insurance coverage

3. Activation Criteria

  • Revenue drops to 50%-75% of the 3-month average: Top 5 placement, 5 social media posts, 5 ad campaigns, and discount vouchers.
  • Revenue drops to 20%-50% of the 3-month average: Top 3 placement, 10 social media posts, 7 ad campaigns, and free shipping vouchers.
  • Revenue drops below 20% of the 3-month average: Top 1 placement, 20 multi-platform posts, 10 ad campaigns, and 100% free shipping.

4. Implementation Process

  • Insurance activates after 3 months of consecutive revenue drop.
  • Support continues until revenue recovers or insurance period ends.
  • Automatic suspension when revenue stabilizes.

5. Conditions and Limitations

  • Applies to restaurants with clear data and valid contracts.
  • Coverage may be suspended for violations or fraud.
  • Disputes resolved according to the contract.

6. Our Commitment

  • Timely, transparent, and professional support.
  • Regular reports on revenue and insurance status.
  • Always supporting restaurants through challenging times.
